What Is This About?

This idea was brought up by students at eMobilis school in Westlands, Kenya. This was due to the fact that a number of them left school very late ,and had no clue of the whereabouts of Westlands.


This proved to be very beneficial to them as many of them got home early enough to spend time with their families. Their safety was also increased against the danger that is presented to them as they travel from school to home at the evening hours.
